About me

I analyze the intersection between users, LLMs, and products.

I'm a Senior Data Analyst specializing in LLM analysis, evaluation, and product decision-making. My work focuses on understanding how users interact with AI systems, how models respond, where failures occur, and how those insights translate into better product decisions.

I approach LLMs not just as models, but as actors in a system. I analyze the junction point where user intent, model behavior, and product constraints meet — treating AI interactions as a form of human-to-human communication that can be measured, segmented, and improved.

Alongside LLM evaluation, I bring a strong foundation in traditional analytics: metrics design, experimentation, behavioral analysis, and business impact measurement. This allows me to connect model-level findings with real product outcomes.

What I do

  • LLM Analysis & Evaluation

    Analyze LLM behavior across intents, reasoning patterns, retrieval quality, and failure modes. Design evaluation frameworks that go beyond accuracy and focus on usefulness and product impact.

  • Product & Behavioral Analytics

    Measure how users interact with AI features, identify friction points, and translate qualitative AI behavior into quantitative signals.

  • Experimentation & Decision Support

    Design experiments and metrics to answer: Should we build this? Did it help? What should we change next?

  • Analytics Infrastructure for AI

    Build data pipelines and schemas that make AI interactions analyzable at scale, especially in Snowflake-based environments.
